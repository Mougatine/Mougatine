Help for SoundRain"

- How to find its client id:

  1. Log in Soundcloud.
  2. Go at the registration page.
  3. Register a new application
  4. Enter a name (whatever, it won't matter).
  5. Copy the Client ID.
  6. Paste it in the dialog box SoundRain opened for you.
  7. Enjoy music ! :)
